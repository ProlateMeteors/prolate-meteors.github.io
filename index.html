About Us

Join us on our journey to simplify how you manage your organizations using technology and data.

ProMet

Prolate Meteors is a South African startup founded in 2021 by Athanaseus Ramaila. We provide companies with solutions to help them run their business efficiently and promoting more growth. The idea behind ProMet, short for Prolate Meteors, is to allow companies to outsource Development power, Marketing resources, and Design work. We also specialise in E-learning and content creation. Students that are part of our programs not only receive online classes and studying material but get the opportunity to be career guided.

    If you answer 'Yes' one of the questions below, then you should contact us. Are you

  • a startup company looking to grow you brand on the online market?
  • an establishment that wants to improve their user experience?
  • a student looking for online learning resources and career guidance?
